Oven Fries

Finished oven fries

Easy and delicious homemade french fries compliment any meal!

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. cut potatoes into 1/2 inch wedges
  3. place wedges on baking sheet
  4. drizzle with olive oil
  5. season generously with salt and pepper
  6. bake 20 minutes and serve!
