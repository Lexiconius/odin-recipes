Grilled Cheese

Finished Grilled Cheese

This delicious take on a classic will leave your fingers less greasy and have you begging for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 thick slice of cheddar
  • 1/4 cup grated havarti

Steps

  1. heat small pan over medium heat
  2. construct sandwich out of bread and cheeses
  3. spread mayonnaise on the outside of both slices of bread
  4. set sandwich in pan and let first side cook until the bread slides freely approx. 2 minutes
  5. flip sandwich and repeat on other side
  6. slice if desired and serve!
