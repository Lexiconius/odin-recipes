Soft Boiled Eggs

Finished Soft Boiled Eggs

Simple soft boiled eggs can be a welcomed midday snack!

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 3 cups water
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. bring water to a boil
  2. add pinch of salt
  3. set all 5 eggs in water
  4. let boil for 2 minutes
  5. deshell eggs, easiest under running cold water
  6. enjoy!
